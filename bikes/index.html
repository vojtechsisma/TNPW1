Colnago

C68 Carbon Road Bike - Super Record EPS - Limited Edition - 2023 - Motoki Yoshio x Colnago

Colnago presents here an icon with a paint scheme that aims to live up to the tradition and uniqueness of the C series. The project was realized by Motoki Yoshio, Milan-based and very prestigious Japanese designer. Colnago is known not only for the quality of its frames, but also for the originality and uniqueness of its finishes. From the construction to the decor, Colnago clearly shows here what is possible when tradition meets innovation. It should not be considered simply a limited edition bike, but a unique piece to collect. Entirely handmade in Italy - born from the collaboration between the company of the "ace of clubs" and one of the most respected contemporary designers in the cycling industry.

Basso

DIAMANTE DISC - Ultegra 8150 Di2 - Carbon Road Bike - 2023 - Fade Opal White

The Basso Diamante Disc convinces with its timeless lines, flawless geometry and the rounded tube shapes, which make the bike even more aerodynamic. Made by hand, the Basso Diamante is an authentic embodiment of a noble Italian road bike. The design language of the Diamante dispenses with superfluous design elements and instead focuses exclusively on its main task: racing.

Trek

ÉMONDA SLR 7 DISC Ultegra Di2 - 2023 - Metallic Red Smoke to Red Carbon Smoke

The Émonda SLR 7 Disc is an ultra-light, aerodynamic carbon road bike and Trek's fastest climber. Along with the legendary ride quality of Trek 's lightest road bike platform, this bike's aerodynamic tubes of lightweight OCLV layup deliver plenty of speed. Plus, you'll benefit from the precision and unmatched shifting speed of the new Shimano Ultegra Di2 wireless electronic drivetrain.
